Uploaded on Aug 19, 2010



This is the "prologue" for a much longer, super-epic video I intend to make as soon as I have a decent amount of season 3 clips to work with (I need something fresh to work with--plus more evil Morgana clips and some new Lancelot ones!) Essentially, this idea was born when I was making my Queen of Camelot video. My original plan was to make a video that encompassed several of the main aspects of the Arthurian legend: the love triangle, evil Morgana, and Mordred versus Arthur and Merlin. Needless to say, it would have turned into an eight minute video if I'd done that, and that just felt like...too much. But now that I've seen the season 3 trailer and I have idea of what's coming, I think it'll be fairly easy to find the inspiration to put something like that together (or make two videos dealing with it). So, we'll see :)
