******The alternative ending of the "World's end"******So, when Jack saw Lizzie and Will happy together he had no doubts because of his choice to become a captain of flying Dutchman. He kills John's heart.After that he becomes an angry and cruel cause he has no heart.He doesn't have any memories about his adventures, about his friends or enemies and about the girl which he had always loved.But ten years have passed and he comes back on the island where is hedden his heart.There he starts to feel something...He feels...love...and...regrets that it has gone. But the time has come. He has to come back on the Dutchman. One day the Dutchman meets a ship.A ship which is called "The black pearl".Will, Gibbs, Pintel, Ragetti are there and...Elizabeth too. Jack is standing right in front of them and doesn't feel anything.In the panic Lizzie sees Jack she's trying to say something but Jack is indifferent. He commands "Fire"...And now his heart is really gone cause SHE is gone...
