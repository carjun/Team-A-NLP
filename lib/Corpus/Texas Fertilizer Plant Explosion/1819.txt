Published on Sep 20, 2013



Meet Big Ben! Big Ben is 74 pounds, a Lab/Collie mix, so his name is perfect. He is a big fella. Ben is between 4 and 5 years old and likes everyone he meets kitties and other dogs included.  Ben  is attentive and wants to please. He's a pretty dog with his yellow coat and sweet brown eyes. Ben loves going for car rides, meeting other dogs and spending time with his humans. Big Ben has a heart of gold and a great face to match. If you are a big dog lover, Come meet Big Ben. Give this dog a chance, and he will prove himself and his devotion! Adopt Big Ben!To adopt Big Ben, please contact One Step Closer Animal Rescue (O.S.C.A.R) at 973-652-3969 or visit their website at http://www.oscaranimalrescue.org/.
