Created by The Shelter Pet Project and funded by Halo Pet Foundation, the campaign encourages potential adopters to consider the shelter as the first place to find a new best friend. Visit www.theshelterpetproject.org to search for available shelter pets in your area and learn more about shelter pet adoption.
