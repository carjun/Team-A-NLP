This is the introduction video to my newest series ... Spreading The Love. Videos posted approximately once a week and in no particular order. The channels will most likely be: spiritual, new aged, pagan, occult, witchcraft, Wiccan orientated.  Each video in this series will highlight a channel that I subscribe to. I will discuss what the theme of the channel is, what I love about the channel, why I subscribed to the channel, what my favorite video is on that channel and other information about the YouTuber and the channel. Join me in spreading the love ...
