The crowning piece of over a year's work! It's NarutoHECK. I am sure some of you have seen Konoha Idol or Funny Naruto Character Theme Songs, but this is different, this is actually edited well. I loved the idea, but was always so upset that they were usually just screenshots and the same stupid songs over and over and over again. At the time I picked these songs, none of them had been used before save for two.
