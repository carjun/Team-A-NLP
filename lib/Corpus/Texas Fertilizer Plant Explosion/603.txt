Ever done something really stupid out of jealousy or anger, then wished you could undo it? Well that's what this video is all about.Hermione is in love with Harry, but he only sees her as a friend. Instead he has feelings for Cho Chang, which makes Hermione extrememly jealous. Everytime she sees him talking with Cho, she has a go at him, not seeming to realise that she is driving him further away.One day her jealousy gets the better of her and she does something, she will later come to bitterly regret.
