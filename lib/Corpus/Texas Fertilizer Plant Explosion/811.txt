Published on Oct 18, 2013



Many of the stories I hear at the hospital come as a shock and not only shake my belief in our health care but also the care provided by KGH.I've talked to a lot of people and the unsatisfied out number the satisfied at KGH by far.  One patient told me that while on the 4th floor he had his nurse call light on for 4 hours before the nurse showed up, when he protested, she told him there are other patients there also.The hospital is crowded, patients in hallways and I've heard from staff that even with the $360 million expansions, the hospital will not have any more beds than before.
