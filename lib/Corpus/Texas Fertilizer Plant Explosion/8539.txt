Black Country Communion is back in the studio recording their 3rd album. Glenn Hughes, Joe Bonamassa, Jason Bonham and Derek Sherinian have come back together to make the highly anticipated 3rd album. After 2 highly successful studio albums, a live CD and a live DVD, BCC is back in the studio to make another Rock masterpiece. Black Country Communion has been called The Greatest Rock Band of the last decade and The Saviors of Rock.
