  You can personalize Google News to focus on what you want. This feature includes Personalized Search, which tailors your Google searches based on your web history and more. Use Personalized Search to view and manage your history of past searches and news articles you've clicked on. (You can turn off Personalized Search or remove items from your Web History at any time.) Get news headlines and search results recommended specifically for you, based on what you've searched for and clicked on. Your personalized Google News is part of your Google Account so you can access it from anywhere by signing in.
