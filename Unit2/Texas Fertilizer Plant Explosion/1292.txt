Published on Dec 20, 2012



2013 marks 500 years since Spanish explorer Juan Ponce de León landed on the upper east coast of Florida. Viva Florida 500 will commemorate that meeting of Old and New worlds by celebrating Florida's native heritage and 500 years of visitors. Join the commemoration by inviting your friends and family to visit Florida at www.sharealittlesunshine.org.
