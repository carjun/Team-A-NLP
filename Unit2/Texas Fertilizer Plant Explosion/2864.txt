Uploaded on Feb 20, 2012



A voyage into the heart of the Oberheim OB-Xa from the year 1980.Exploration by Marko Ettlich (RetroSound)Polyphony - 4, 6 or 8 voices | curtis chip module based.It has been used by Van Halen (Jump), Gary Numan, Prince, New Order, Depeche Mode, Queen, Supertramp, Simple Minds, Chicago, Rush, Bon Jovi and many more!Oscillators - 2 VCO's per voice (sawtooth or pulse) LFO - 1 LFO Filter - 2 Pole & 4 Pole lowpass with ADSR envelope VCA - 1 ADSR VCA envelope Keyboard - 61 note Memory - 32 patches Control - CV / Gate on voice 1 only
