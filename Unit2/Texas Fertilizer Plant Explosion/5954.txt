Gilles is a year old white male Bichon/Poodle mix that is a great boy with lots of love to give. Gilles is content to snuggle on your lap, likes to go for walks, but doesn't particularly need a great deal of exercise! He is a true love bug and so handsome! Gilles was found as a stray and really likes being with people. If you are looking for a small dog that loves and sweet and very, very handsome...Gilles is your guy!  ADOPT GILLES!
