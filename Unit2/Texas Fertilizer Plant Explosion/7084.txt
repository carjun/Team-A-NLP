Published on Oct 14, 2012



So yeah, this contains spoilers for an episode that wasn't officially released on tv but was released on the internet. I'm sure if anything will get me a strike, this will, but whatever.This video is not great at all. I am sorry I uploaded something this... ugh... when it has been forever since I've uploaded ANYTHING of substance.But the newest episode of Young Justice was the most heart wrenching yet and I've been dreading the moment M'gann got her hands on Kaldur. She is my favorite character and still is. She has made mistakes, ones that will cost her dearly, but what happened is not just her fault... I wanted to get her pain across.So it's just a simple video with badly placed voiceovers and thrown in clips to some instrumental music. Mostly a vent video of what's been happening in YJ.Either way, it did make me feel better sooooo to be honest, that's what matters 'cause I love my precious baby martian and I needed to vent for her.
