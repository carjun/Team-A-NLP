Published on Sep 16, 2012



This is a general Tarot Reading for my client, Mary, in regards to her relationship with C. She also had some specific questions: What is the final outcome between C. and I? Where do we stand? Will he ever find me in his heart? Will he ever chose me, love me in return? Will he ever see me for what I am worth?I used the Tarot of the New Visions deck for this Reading. You can find them on Amazon - http://www.aeclectic.net/tarot/cards/...If you are interested in receiving a Tarot Reading from CricketSong please visit her website at: http://www.lunarwisdom.net
