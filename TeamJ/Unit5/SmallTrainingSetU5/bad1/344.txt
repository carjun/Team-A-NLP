            Newtown school shooting: Your thoughts
        

 People across the world are sharing their concern, anger and sadness after one of the deadliest school shootings took place Friday in Connecticut. Suspected gunman Adam Lanza opened fire in an elementary school, killing 26 people, 20 of them children, according to police. What are your thoughts following the shooting? Whether you want to share a message of hope for the victims, your thoughts about gun control, or sound off on another related topic, this is the place. CNN will continue to report on the school shootings, and your thoughts may be part of our coverage. If you have tributes to the victims of the shootings, we invite you to share them here.
