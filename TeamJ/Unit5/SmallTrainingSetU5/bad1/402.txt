Comments Policy

Close window

Please feel free to add a comment to our forums and articles. The rules are simple:

Keep it clean. No swears or porn.

Be nice. No threats, abuse, or spam.

Be respectful. No hate speech.

Be truthful. Don't pretend to be somebody else.

Debate, but don't attack. Stay away from personal attacks.

No ads. No pushing a product or service, and no asking for money.

Don't steal. Don't post somebody else's stuff, and don't libel anybody.

We can edit. Or delete. Or ban you.
