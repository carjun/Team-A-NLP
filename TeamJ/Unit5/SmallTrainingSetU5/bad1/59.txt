Published on Dec 15, 2012



Prime Minister Manmohan Singh raised the issue of pending trial against perpetrators of 26/11 Mumbai terror attacks based in Pakistan with the visiting Interior Minister Rehman Malik. Malik, who had a 15-minute meeting with the Prime Minister, said a Pakistani judicial commission will be sent here to take forward the 26/11 case and efforts would be made to conclude the trial in Pakistan as soon as possible.For more log on to http://www.newsx.com
