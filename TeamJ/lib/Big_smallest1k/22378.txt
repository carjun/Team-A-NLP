The majority of respondents are against the war and believe there is no reason behind the open-ended war. They even not believe the war is irrelevant to the terrorism issues, essentially raised by the Bush administration. Two of the prominent guests give a brief account of the 2001 war and say the US does not have any evidence that Al-Qaeda carried out the 911 attack. Interestingly, the British public does not even know the difference between the Taliban and al-Qaeda.
