WTF?2010 September 6tags: Arizona, civil liberties, privacyby Cowboy DreTempe residents asked to register cellphones for emergencies | AZ CentralThe Tempe Police Department is urging residents to register their cellphones for the emergency system that lets people know about emergencies.If you have a home phone, you already have the feature. All landline phones in Maricopa County have been part of the system since 2004, said Molly Enright, police community affairs specialist.What is new is that Tempe residents  and, in fact, residents from across Maricopa County  have the option to provide their public-safety agencies the cellphones registered to themselves and their families.The capability to get emergency messages on cellphones became available last week, Enright said.
