Attendance estimates for the Wadena rock fest started at 30,000 but ran as high (no pun intended) as 50,000 for the three-day event, July 31-Aug. 2, 1970.Well, OK: If you stop to eat a Barney Burger at the downtown bar in Wadena, you might notice photos (of naked hippies bathing in the Volga River) and posters from the 1970 fest on the wall.My Sunday column (July 25, 2010) will feature a remembrance of Wadena.The former Clarence Schmitt farm southwest of town isnt necessarily difficult to find.
