Despite all our efforts, the satirical newspaper reported, the U.S. government has proven no match for the awesome power of the illegal high.Funny stuff. But a dozen years later, serious news outlets are writing pretty much the same story.After 40 years, the Associated Press reported this month, the United States war on drugs has cost $1 trillion and hundreds of thousands of lives, and for what?
