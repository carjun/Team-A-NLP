Go Phish | Shore News TodaySummertime is here, and the waves are gone. The sun is shining almost every day, and the air and water temperatures are considerably high for the time of year. However, as many surfers know, around here summer is probably the worst time for waves. Thats not to say that we dont get lucky sometimes, but I feel that almost every year since I can remember, the ocean has been flat in the beginning of the summer.Whats a surfer to do? Where does one go to recharge ones soul when the ocean is without the surfers gift? Well, we are particularly lucky here in South Jersey to be in the heart of summer concert Valhalla. The combination of great bands, great venues and perfect weather can make up for the lack of waves.
