Witnesses and medics say at least eight other people were wounded late Friday and early Saturday when Israeli warplanes carried out a series of retaliatory strikes in Gaza.  Several buildings and an alleged weapons-smuggling tunnel were hit. Earlier Friday, a rocket fired from the Gaza Strip landed in the southern Israeli city of Ashkelon.  The rocket blew out apartment windows and damaged cars, but no injuries were reported. Israel announced Friday it is filing a complaint about the Ashkelon attack and a mortar fire incident last week to the U.N.
