They argue that state legalization of marijuana would violate the Constitutions Supremacy Clause, which says this Constitution, and the Laws of the United States which shall be made in Pursuance thereofshall be the supreme Law of the Land. Since Prop. 19 conflicts with the federal Controlled Substances Act, they say, it is unconstitutional.
