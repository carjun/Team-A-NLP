The team is open to Arizona youth between the ages of 14 and 20. Team members shoot local matches during the season (Sept - May), practice like crazy June & July, and participate at the National Rifle Matches at Camp Perry, Ohio in August. Most equipment including AR-15 rifles, calibered in .223, are provided free of charge.
