At least three people are reported to have been killed and many more injured. Loud explosions were reported near a hotel where Mr Ouattara and his administration are based. A spokesman for Mr Ouattara told the BBC they were being attacked.  Mr Ouattara's supporters are trying to wrest power from the incumbent President, Laurent Gbagbo, who is refusing to accept defeat in last month's election. The UN Secretary-General, Ban Ki-Moon, on Wednesday warned that civil war could return to Ivory Coast if the political stalemate continues.
