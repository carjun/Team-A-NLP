Not Bean Bags Again!2010 October 9tags: hippie furnitureby Cowboy DreBeanbags are cool again, baby | Ottawa CitizenBeanbag chairs of the 60s conjure up images of hippies mellowing out on the floor, listening to groovy music on headphones by the light of a lava lamp.Todays beanbag is about versatility, comfort and eco-smarts.Two Ottawa entrepreneurs discovered the modern version of the chair while poolside in Dominican Republic. It was love at first lounge.We ended up buying a couple of them and never turned back, says Linda Goodwin, who co-founded the newly created Lotus Lounger with her husband Chico.The chairs are ideal for an urban or outdoor lifestyle, whether in the living room for watching TV, in the spare room as an extra bed, or a dorm room for studying.
