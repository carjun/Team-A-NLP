Investigators were trying to determine if officers may have fired on a fellow officer whose badge and other identifying markings came off his uniform during the scuffle, he said.No arrests have been made, but dozens of people were being questioned, Guglielmi said.Authorities said officers arrived at the Select Lounge to find a large fight and called for backup.
