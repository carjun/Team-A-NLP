Bobby Jindal said.All 13 people have been accounted for, said Petty Officer Bill Colclough of the Coast Guard. They were found floating on a raft, officials said. Mariner Energy, which owns the Vermilion Oil Rig 380, said none of the crew members was hurt in the incident, despite earlier reports of a single injured worker.Also, Mariner indicated that the fire  which was first reported to the Coast Guard by workers on a nearby rig around 9:20 a.m. (10:20 a.m. ET)  was not sparked by an explosion.
