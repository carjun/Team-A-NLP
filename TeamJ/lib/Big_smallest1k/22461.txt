Two other Western troops, an Afghan soldier and an American civilian were injured, NATOs International Security Assistance Force said in a statement.But an ISAF official, speaking on condition of anonymity because he was not authorized to comment publicly, said the crash took place in Zabul province, a Taliban stronghold.
