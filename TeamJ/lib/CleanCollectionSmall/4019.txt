Images: 27 dead in Connecticut school shootingWitnesses reported hearing hundreds of gunshots, many of which were fired in a kindergarten classroom. Connecticut State Police Lt. Paul Vance said the shooting was contained to two rooms in one section of the school.Vance said 18 children werepronounceddead at the scene, and two others died at a nearby hospital. All six adult victims, including the principal, were pronounced dead at the scene.Only one person who was shot during the rampage survived, and was being treated at Danbury Hospital. A dispatcher at the Newtown Volunteer Ambulance Corps told the Associated Press that the injured person was a teacher who had been shot in the foot.Lanza killed his mother in her Yogananda Street home in Newtown on Friday morning before going to the school, the Associated Press reported.Citing law enforcement sources, the Associated Press reported thata Glock and a Sig Sauer, both high-caliber pistols, were found inside the school, and a .223-caliber rifle was found in the back of Lanza's mother's car, the vehicle he used to get to the school Friday morning.A law enforcement official told the Associated Press that Adam Lanza's brother, Ryan, 24, of Hoboken, N.J., had been extremely cooperative and was not believed to have any involvement in the rampage. Officials were searching Ryan Lanza's computers and phone records as part of the investigation, but he was not under arrest or in custody.
