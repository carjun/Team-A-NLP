Smith led the Bears to the Super Bowl in 2006, where they lost to the Indianapolis Colts. He was named the Associated Press NFL Coach of the Year in 2005. Smith came to Chicago from St. Louis (2001-03), where he served as defensive coordinator. In 2001, he helped the Rams return to the Super Bowl after missing the playoffs the previous season. Smith previously coached linebackers for the Tampa Bay Buccaneers (1996-2000).  Background: Smith played at Tulsa (1976-79), where he was a linebacker before moving to strong safety and earning two-time All-America and three-time All-Missouri Conference defensive back honors. Began his coaching career at his hometown high school (Big Sandy, Texas) in 1980 before moving to Cascia Hall Prep in Tulsa the following year. Two years later Smith began coaching collegiately at Tulsa (1983-86), Wisconsin (1987), Arizona State (1988-1991), Kentucky (1992), Tennessee (1993-94), and Ohio State (1995).  Personal: Born May 8, 1958, in Gladewater, Texas.
