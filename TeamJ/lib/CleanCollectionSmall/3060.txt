Was she the mother of a schoolkid? Was she the daughter of an administrator?She is Jillian Soto, the sister of Victoria Soto, the first-grade teacher who has been hailed as a hero for coming between her first-grade students and the shooter by shepherding them into a closet. Victoria Soto, 27, was killed.Scores of people gathered in the slain teacher's hometown of Stratford, Conn., Saturday night for an emotional vigil.The iconic Associated Press photo was taken Friday, as relatives descended on the school to find out about their loved ones.
