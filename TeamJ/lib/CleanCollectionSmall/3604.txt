The person spoke on condition of anonymity because the investigation was still under way.It wasn't clear how many people were injured at Sandy Hook Elementary School in Newtown.A dispatcher at the Newtown Volunteer Ambulance Corps said a teacher had been shot in the foot and taken to Danbury Hospital.
