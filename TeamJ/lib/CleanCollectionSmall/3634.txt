According to NBC NY, the shooter is dead.Witnesses say shots could be heard during morning announcements at the school, which prompted teachers to move students to the corners of their classrooms, reports NBC Conn.A reverse 911 call alerted parents to the shooting.The Newtown Bee reports that children were escorted out of Sandy Hook Elementary under police guard. The school district issued a lockdown of all schools in the area until officials could garner more information about the incident.According to NBC Conn., hospital representatives in Danbury said three people were admitted to the facility from the school.The Associated Press reports that one of those injured and being treated at the hospital is a teacher who was shot in the foot.The Bee reports one child, apparently seriously injured, was carried out of the school by a police officer.The district has cancelled mid-day kindergarten.Newtown is located about 80 miles northeast of New York City.Want to read more Crime Examiner articles?
