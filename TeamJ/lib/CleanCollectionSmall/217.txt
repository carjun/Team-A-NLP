With the Sandy Hook Elementary School Victims Relief Fund anyone can donate a big or small amount of money to benefit and gather support for the victims, families, and all others affected locally by the tragic shooting.There has been over $93,000 already raised for Sandy Hook Elementary through the Crowdrise website. Crowdrise partners with Network for Good processing all donations. Network for Good will send donations, less transaction fees, directly to PTSA Sandy Hook.  Donations made in December will be sent no later than Jan. 15, 2012.With 20 children and 6 adults involved in this sad incident, the school and the community need help in healing. With even a small donation, the elementary school will be sure to provide the services they can to the survivors and victims families.VehicleHistory.us.org and their staff send out their condolences to those lost in the Sandy Hook Elementary and the town of Newton, CT. Hopefully with enough help from this fund and others similar the families and community will be able to heal.Other charities and organizations helping with the Sandy Hook tragedy are Sandy Hook School Support Fund through United Way, Danbury Hospital, Newtown Memorial Fund, Newtown Youth and Family Services, Newtown Parent Connection, Project Linus, and The American Red Cross.About VehicleHistory.us.org:At VehicleHistory.us.org they take pride in providing most comprehensive vehicle history reports in United States and Canada. With hundreds of users daily, motor vehicle report availability and customer service are top priorities for VehicleHistory.us.org.
