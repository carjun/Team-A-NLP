More details will be released once the medical examiner hascompleted his investigation, Vance said. He said detectives,including three separate teams, are still working at the crimescene. The gunman forced his way into the school, Vance said. Allthe recovered weapons were found near the suspect, he said.Vance said evidence has been found that may answer why thiscrime was committed. The lieutenant also said no other arrests associated withthe crime were made.
