Sammie Eaglebear Chavez tried to recruit other students to assist him with carrying out a plan to lure students into the school auditorium where he planned to begin shooting them after chaining the doors shut. He also told them that if the students assisting him did not do what they were supposed to do, he would not hesitate to kill them and/or himself.
