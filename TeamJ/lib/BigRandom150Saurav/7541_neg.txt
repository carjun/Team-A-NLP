Plot, Holder Says | The New York TimesMore firmly and publicly than before, senior officials of the Obama administration on Sunday blamed the failed attempt to blow up a bomb in Times Square on the Pakistani Taliban, an accusation that should increase pressure on the Pakistan military to attack the organization in its bastions in the lawless tribal region of North Waziristan.Attorney General Eric Holder, appearing on NBCs Meet the Press, said the Taliban in Pakistan directed this plot and may have also financed it.
