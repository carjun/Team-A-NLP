For every visible hippy, barefoot, flowered, beaded, there are a thousand invisible members of the turned-on underground. Persons whose lives are tuned in to their inner vision, who are dropping out of the TV comedy of American Life.Timothy Leary (The Politics of Ecstasy) 1967So youre kicking back, life is pretty good. Your kids are grown, work is good, maybe youre even retired.Heading toward the golden years. Your thoughts these days are more nostalgic. Ahhh remember when?Well, snap yourself out of it.With the current state of the world, hippies have more to be concerned about than ever. Cant hear the call? Turn up your hearing aid. Oil sources are running low, glaciers are melting, and the Earths temperature is quickly changing. If humans continue to live with the same level of disregard, it will create extreme global climates. The temperature of New York will be like the temperature of South Carolina, and habitats and homes will be destroyed.The implications of hippies concerns are vast, and they cannot be ignored. Here in the U.S. civil rights have almost vanished. The Man has subverted our Constitution i.e The Patriot Act. He has complete control over educating our children No child left behind. And Torture is acceptable. Enhanced Interrogations. Wall Street owns everything including our Politicians. -CITIZENS UNITED v. FEDERAL ELECTION COMMISSION decision No. 08205. Argued March 24, 2009Reargued September 9, 2009Decided January 21, 2010.Through political and social activism, hippies can spread their beliefs and solutions throughout society.So stop your Flash backing to the old days. This way change may hopefully be made. If you thought Max Yasgurs Farm was a big gathering place, the internet is a worldwide Commune. Hippies can communicate with others around the world to enhance and support each others activism. Hippies can use the internet to publish statistics , as well as solutions..
