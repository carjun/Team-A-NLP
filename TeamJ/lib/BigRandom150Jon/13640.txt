(AP) - Senior Darryl Shazier scored a career-high 28 points and Bucknell held off Navy 71-64 on Saturday night in the Patriot League opener for both teams.The Bison (10-7) snapped a four-game Navy winning streak in the series. Bucknell has won eight of its last nine and is 1-0 in the conference for the first time since 2007-08.Shazier was 10 of 17 from the field and hit 7 of 8 free throws.The Midshipmen led by eight in the first half, but the Bison used a 9-2 spurt to trail 28-27 at the break.The second half was tied four times. At 39-39, Bucknell went on a 6-0 run to take the lead for good.Navy (5-12) got within two points on three occasions, the last time at 62-60 with 2:39 to go.The Bison hit 9 of 10 free throws to seal the win.Mike Muscala added 19 points for Bucknell and Joe Willman had 12.J.J. Avila paced the Midshipmen with 20 points.Copyright 2011 The Associated Press. All rights reserved.
