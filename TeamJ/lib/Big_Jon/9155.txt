Iran plane crashes, 105 are feared dead    Tehrn :  Iran|Jan 09, 2011By Claire87        1 0     Views: 26      On Sunday, an Iranian passenger plane crashed near Orumieh in the Azerbaijan province in northwestern Iran. All passengers and crew members are feared dead in the Iran plane crash. The plane had 105 people on board; both passengers and crew members. The plane that crashed in Iran was a Boeing 727 belonging to the national-state airline, Iran Air. The Iran Air plane crashed into a valley due to bad weather and heavy fog. Heavy snow and fog is severely hampering rescue efforts that have been underway after the Iran Air plane crash. Some news sources are reporting 50 people to have survived the Iran Air plane crash; however, no confirmation has yet been received about the number of actual deaths.
