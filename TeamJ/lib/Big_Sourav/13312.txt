Shop e-Readers Is Open For Business Selling eBook Readers     Orange :  CA :  USA|Oct 18, 2010By albertoste        1 0     Views: 110       (1888PressRelease) Purchase the new Kindle Graphite WiFI or 3G! Check out others too, like the Barnes and Noble Nook WIFI, Kobo WIFI and more! Shop e-Readers has opened their virtual doors for business selling major name brand e-readers such as Amazon, Barnes and Noble, Kobo, and Sony. They also are now carrying major accessories such as executive leather cases, decals and pouch cases. Shop e-Readers is a local Vancouver, British ColumbiaVancouver, British Columbia based company specializing in electronic readers and technical support. Many major e-reader manufacturers are happy with just getting a sale and that is it. We provide long term support for your e-reader via our support partner Good e-Reader.  Good e-Reader provides the latest news, reviews and previews on their news blog; they also provide book clubs, guides, tutorials, video tutorials, firmware updates and news on every major electronic reader. Shop e-Readers will sell the e-readers and Good e-Reader provides all of the long term technical support. All of Shop e-Readers products come with an instructional DVD that will teach the end user how to setup, configure, load ebooks, and learn all of the major steps involved in getting the most of your investment. They also provide 100 free eBooks that are compatible with every e-reader in their inventory. Shop e-Reader carries the Amazon Kindle 3 Graphite WIFI, the Amazon Kindle WIFI/3G, the Barnes and Noble nook Wi-Fi and 3G model. They also carry the Kobo, Kobo Wireless and new Sony e-Readers.  About Shop e-Reader Shop e-Readers have been in business for two years, serving the local Vancouver, British Columbia market.
