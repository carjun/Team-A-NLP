The city is located at 1000 km northwest from here, in the mountains. TV reports said that the plane was smashed into pieces, but did not explode. State television said the plane was going to Urumiyeh from Tehran. Air crashes are frequent in Iran due to sanctions imposed by the US on purchase of aircraft and spare parts from the western countries.
