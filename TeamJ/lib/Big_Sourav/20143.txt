"We are very concerned with the start of demolition of the Shepherd Hotel in East Jerusalem, "Clinton said after arriving in Abu Dhabi in the early hours of Monday (local time) to discuss the peace process in the Middle East. "This troubling case undermines the peace efforts to achieve a solution based on two states, Israel and Palestine, said in charge of U.S. diplomacy in a statement. "Especially, this fact undermines the logic of a reasonable and necessary agreement between the parties on the status of Jerusalem, " he added. The Israeli authorities began demolishing Sunday morning a part of the old hotel Shepherd, the Palestinian neighborhood of Sheikh Jarrah in East Jerusalem. On site will be built a complex of 20 apartments for Jewish settlers.
