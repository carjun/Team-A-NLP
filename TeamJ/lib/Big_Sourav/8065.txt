Avondale :  AZ :  USA|Nov 21, 2010By Michelle Lincaster        2 0     Views: 937       The Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon 2010 is going to officially start on the 21st of November 2010 at Tempe Beach Park. The Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon 2010 is a prestigious event for any athlete to attend and an even greater accomplishment if anyone nails it. Comprising of biking, running and swimming, the Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon 2010 is quite a challenge for an ordinary runner let alone for someone who has been told by the doctors that he wont be able to walk. Chance Leonard was 15 years old when he suffered a fracture in his spine and since then have had 3 surgeries. By participating in the Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon 2010, he hopes to raise $100 for every mile he walks. Now the running is a total of 16.2 miles, the biking stretch is for 112-miles and the swimming is 2.4 miles- a total of $14000 if Chance Leonard is able to go through the challenges of the Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon 2010.        1 of 51         Amateur triathletes start the swim portion of the Ford Ironman World Championship triathlon in Kailua-Kona      Michelle Lincaster is based in Boston, Massachusetts, United States of America,and is Anchor for Allvoices Report Credibility           Share:                             Credibility      Reach             Global Reach This report's viewers come from the followingcountries:  United States of America (79.8%) India (11.9%) China (1.6%) Pakistan (1.0%) 13 other countries (4.8%)           READ MORE: Ironman Arizona, sports-news, Avondale, Ironman, hawaii, Ironman Triathlon, Ironman World Championship, Individual sports, triathlon, Ford Ironman Arizona Triathlon   More News From: Avondale :  AZ :  USA     Advertisement      Post a Comment Comments: 0                  Enter your comment below       Add Your Comment  Save Changes  Cancel              More From Avondale          Answer to question 'is Facebook really shutting down?' is No!
