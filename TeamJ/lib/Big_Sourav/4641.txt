The Playbook is powered by Research In Motions new tablet operating system, a 7-inch touchscreen size, two cameras, front and rear facing, and can play high-definition video of up to 1080p quality. The Blackberry Playbook can also access the internet via WiFi and supports bluetooth connection. RIMs new tablet is scheduled to hit the United States stores via Sprints wireless network exclusively. The price of Blackberry Playbook starts at $399 (8GB). Motorola Xoom, debuted today at CES in Las Vegas for the first time, represents the first real Android tablet sanctioned by Google. This new device has an Android operating system that is specially designed for tablets, the Honeycomb or version 3.0 of Googles popular mobile OS. Motorola Xooms specification: This new tablet uses CDMA, with 10.1-inch touchscreen size that has a resolution of 1280800. It includes a 5 megapixel HD rear camera, and a front facing camera too. Xoom is powered by Dual-core NVIDIA Tegra 2 and 1024 MB RAM. Motorolas tablet also supports WiFi and bluetooth connectivity, USB and the 32 GB internal storage is expandable of up to 64 GB by using microSD, microSDHC. This Motorola device is a Verizon Wireless exclusive tablet. So theres a possibility that this will be displayed with the iPad 2 in all Verizon stores. The price of Motorola Xoom was not revealed.        1 of 26         Galapagos e-Media Tablets by Sharp Electronics are displayed during a news conference at 2011 International CES in Las Vegas      Rashid Hussain is based in Multn, Punjab, Pakistan,and is Reporter for Allvoices Report Credibility           Share:                             Credibility      Reach             Global Reach This report's viewers come from the followingcountries:  United States of America (50.5%) Canada (11.7%) Pakistan (7.1%) India (4.9%) 21 other countries (22.6%)           READ MORE: Technology Internet, BlackBerry PlayBook, Motorola&rsquo, technology-news, Las Vegas, Embedded Linux, Mobile software, Android, Blackberry, smartphones, Motorola, technology-news, Motorola Xoom&, computing   More News From: Las Vegas :  NV :  USA     Advertisement      Post a Comment Comments: 0                  Enter your comment below       Add Your Comment  Save Changes  Cancel              Related Allvoices Contributions         (Video) Motorola Xoom Android Tablet - iPad's worst nightmare?
