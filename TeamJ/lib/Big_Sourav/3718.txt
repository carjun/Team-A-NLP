According to the reports atleast 50 people have survived. It was flying from the capital of Iran, Tehran, when it came down near the north-western city of Orumiyeh. The media said that Orumiyeh was its final destination. The plane crashed near Orumiyeh 430 miles north-west of Tehran around 19:45 local time (1615 GMT). The Govt representative said that the plane took off about an hour later than its scheduled and it was coming down due to the bad weather and snowfall.
