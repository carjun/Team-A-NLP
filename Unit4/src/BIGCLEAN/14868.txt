['At least 119 people were killed when a massive fire broke out in poultry processing plant in Jilin province. More than 50 people were injured.', 'The Jilin Baoyuanfeng Poultry Plant employs about 1,200 people. An estimated 350 workers were on site when the fire began.', 'Xinhua reported the fire was sparked by three early morning explosions.
