Published on Oct 18, 2013



Looking for a puppy? Meet Rascal! A three month old terrier mix that is fun, cute and a great pup! Rascal is small to medium in size and is a typical energetic, loving young pup. He is the last one in his litter that is looking for his forever family and would love to be with a family that can spend time with him and give him the attention and training that a young pup needs. Rascal has his age appropriate shots, and is all ready to be a part of a great pack of humans! And he is super cute!! ADOPT RASCAL!
