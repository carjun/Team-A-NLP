Published on Dec  7, 2012



TONI & GUY HAIRDRESSING ACADEMY AVANT-GARDE HOUSE OF STYLE  premiered as a LIVE photo-shoot and LIVE Model Exhibit in the fall 11 - featuring emerging Avant-Garde Designers for ORANGE COUNTY FASHION WEEK.  OC FASHION WEEK℠ also shared the night's festivities with multi-talented haute fashion photographers to debut the works of Toni & Guy's Academy students whose artistic avant-garde endeavors were featured on the catapulting glass CatWalk debut at the iconic restaurant AnQi by Crustacean the favorable fashion hot spot for culinary couture.  Coming Soon to OC FASHION WEEK℠ 2013 The AVANT-GARDE HOUSE OF STYLE II  presented by Toni & Guy Hairdressing Academy, AnQi byCrustacean, RIVIERA MAGAZINE, Hauteoc Traveler & more.
