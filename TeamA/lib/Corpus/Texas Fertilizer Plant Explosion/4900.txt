Fertilizer facility explodes in central TexasA massive explosion on Wednesday night at a fertilizer facility in West, Tex., triggered widespread damage and injuries. On Friday, Mayor Tommy Muska of West, a farm town 20 miles north of Waco, said that 14 bodies had been recovered, including those of five volunteer firefighters and four emergency medical technicians.
