I promised them that if they hit 150,000 views I would upload my latest video. Someone made a comment that made me falter in my resolve and I caved, only 2,000 views before the end. It is a video showing how he feels about it all and his relationship to the Kyuubi inside of him. I absolutely love ths song choice and I really want to finish the video someday. To my RL fans that were so dedicated, here you go with my deepest gratitude.
