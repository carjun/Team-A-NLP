Published on Sep  4, 2013



Purina® Cat Chow® celebrated 50 years of feeding great relationships by helping improve the lives of cats all across the nation through their 50 Years / 50 Shelters project. Hundreds of volunteers, dozens of contractors, and thousands of cat people joined together with Purina Cat Chow over 4 months, all in an amazing effort to help homeless cats everywhere. Their efforts built new temporary homes, provided play structures and toys, and simplified cat adoption processes at 50 separate cat shelters aross the US.See how the 50 Years / 50 Shelters project created some unforgettable moments while improving the quality of life for thousands of cats, and find out more at http://www.CatChow.com and http://www.Facebook.com/PurinaCatChow.
