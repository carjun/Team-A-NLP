Published on Feb 23, 2013



I can't believe I finished it in one day (took me about 12 hours though). I guess it was some inspiration of mine, that reached over 9000. I'm very pleased with the result, hope guys you will enjoy it as well. Maskingfest, btw. :P!Kinda missed my videos with a storyline, therefore decided on one. Pretty much goes with the lyrics (http://www.azlyrics.com/lyrics/brandn...), won't explain it in detail though, I'm quite sure some of you may figure it out yourselves :)...Man, I really can't get enough of KisaIta, this has been like OTP of mine for real for so many years . Funny thing, it's one of the most canon pairings in Naruto, the number of hints towards KI is just overwhelming. Good for me, I guess :).And that song... One of my favs for sure. Hardly ever person may hear the track filled with so many emotions. Quite negative ones, as since it's about death and rejection but still... I simply love it.Program used: Sony Vegas Pro 11Song: name of it at the end of the videoThanks for watching and for rating/leaving a comment in advance :)Ahh... And video blocked in Germany, too bad...p.s. Check 1:44-1:46, today I discovered that KisaIta clip (even though I knew already all of them), goddamn, so cute! That eye-contact ;)
